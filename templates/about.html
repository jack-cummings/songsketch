About Song Sketch AI

Song Sketch AI uses cutting edge generative AI to create beautiful, one of a kind artwork based on your playlists. Each image is completely unique and is fully available for you to share, print or distribute. Just remember to tag @songSketchAI on IG!

The Tech

Song Sketch AI uses OpenAI's Dalle to generate images using text prompts. The text prompt templates are modified to include keywords from the user's playlist song titles. Of all the words included in your song titles, the app uses zero-shot learning with Huggingface's BERT LLM to identify words that represent tangible objects, and selects those to draw.
